### The Enterprise Startup Operating System

Crudier is a next-generation Startup Operating System that combines:

* ServiceNow-style Workflow Automation
* Salesforce Lightning CRM
* Enterprise Workspace Management
* Attendance & Productivity Tracking
* Team Collaboration
* Knowledge Management
* Incident Management
* Project & Milestone Tracking

* Advanced Reporting & Analytics

Built for:

* Startups
* Agencies
* Student Organizations
* Enterprise Teams
* SaaS Businesses
* Internal Operations Teams

---

## ✨ Vision

Most organizations use multiple disconnected tools:

* Slack for communication
* Notion for documentation
* Jira for tasks
* Salesforce for CRM
* ServiceNow for workflows
* Google Sheets for reporting

Crudier brings these capabilities into a unified platform where teams can manage operations, projects, sales, workflows, attendance, meetings, knowledge, and analytics from a single workspace.
